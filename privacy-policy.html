Overview

02 — Data Collection

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Contact information — only if you email us (name, email address).
  • Feedback & correspondence — messages you send to us.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device identifiers — Advertising ID (GAID/IDFA), device model, OS version.
  • Usage data — app sessions, features used, crash logs.
  • Network data — IP address, approximate location (country/city level).
  • Ad interaction data — impressions, clicks, and performance metrics from our ad partners.

We do not collect: precise GPS location, biometric data, financial information, health data, contacts, call logs, or any sensitive personal data.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

05 — Children's Privacy

Children's Privacy

For any app directed at children:

  • We configure AdMob to serve only non-personalised, family-safe ads (Google Play Families Policy compliant).
  • We configure AppLovin to serve only child-directed content compliant with COPPA.
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13, please contact us immediately and we will delete it promptly.
