01 — Introduction

Overview

Welcome to AsimAppFactory ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use our mobile applications and visit our website at asimappfactory.github.io.

We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about this policy or our practices, please contact us using the details at the bottom of this page.

By downloading or using any of our apps, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you disagree with any terms, please discontinue use of our applications.

This policy is designed to comply with:

  • Google Play Store Developer Programme Policies
  • Google AdMob Advertising Policies
  • AppLovin Network Policies
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
02 — Data Collection

Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

Information You Provide

  • Contact information — only if you email us or fill a support form (name, email address).
  • Feedback & correspondence — messages you send to us.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device identifiers — Advertising ID (GAID/IDFA), device model, OS version.
  • Usage data — app sessions, features used, crash logs.
  • Network data — IP address, approximate location (country/city level derived from IP).
  • Ad interaction data — impressions, clicks, and ad performance metrics collected by our ad partners.

We do not collect: precise GPS location, biometric data, financial information, health data, contacts, call logs, or any sensitive personal data.

03 — Use of Information

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve our applications.
  • Serve advertisements through Google AdMob and AppLovin (including personalised ads where consent is given).
  • Analyse app usage and diagnose technical issues via crash reporting tools.
  • Respond to your questions and support requests.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

Personalised vs Non-Personalised Ads

Where required by law (e.g., in the EEA/UK), we request your consent before serving personalised ads. If you decline or withdraw consent, you will still see ads but they will not be tailored to your interests. You can change your preference at any time in the app settings.

Opt-out of personalised ads:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → "Delete advertising ID" or "Opt out of Ads Personalisation".
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising → Turn off "Personalised Ads".
  • AppLovin opt-out: applovin.com/optout
05 — Third-Party Services

Third-Party Services & Data Sharing

We may share information with third parties only in these limited circumstances:

  • Ad networks — as described in Section 04 above.
  • Analytics providers — anonymised, aggregated usage statistics to help us understand how our apps are used (e.g., Firebase Analytics).
  • Legal compliance — when required by law, court order, or government authority.
  • Business transfers — in the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred (we will notify you beforehand).

All third-party partners are contractually required to handle your data in compliance with applicable privacy laws, including GDPR where applicable.

Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes in this policy or as required by law. Advertising ID data processed by our ad partners is governed by their respective retention policies.

06 — Children's Privacy

Children's Privacy

Some of our apps may be designed for a general audience including children under 13. For any app directed at children:

  • We configure Google AdMob to serve only non-personalised, family-safe ads (Google Play Families Policy compliant).
  • We configure AppLovin to serve only child-directed content that complies with COPPA.
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 beyond what is required to serve appropriate ads.
  • We do not allow interest-based advertising in apps targeted at children.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13 without proper consent, please contact us immediately and we will delete it promptly.

For apps rated for ages 13+, we comply with applicable laws regarding minors and do not knowingly collect data from users under 13 in jurisdictions where that is prohibited.

07 — Security

Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organisational measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

In the event of a data breach that affects your rights and freedoms, we will notify you and the appropriate authorities as required by law.
