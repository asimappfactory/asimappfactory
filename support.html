FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Are your apps free?
Yes, all our apps are completely free to download and use. They are supported by advertising through Google AdMob and AppLovin.
How do I opt out of personalised ads?
On Android: go to Settings → Google → Ads → "Delete advertising ID". On iOS: Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising → Turn off "Personalised Ads". You can also opt out via AppLovin's opt-out page.
Do you sell my personal data?
No. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
How do I request deletion of my data?
Email us at privacy@asimappfactory.github.io with your request. We will process it within 30 days as required by applicable law.
Are your apps safe for children?
For apps directed at children, we serve only non-personalised, family-safe ads compliant with Google Play Families Policy and COPPA. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
I found a bug — how do I report it?
Please email us at support@asimappfactory.github.io with a description of the issue, your device model, and Android version. Screenshots are helpful too!
How long until I get a reply?
We aim to respond to all enquiries within 24–48 hours on business days. Privacy-related requests are handled within 30 days.
